About Lahore

Lahore is the cultural heart of Pakistan and one of the country's most historic cities. It is famous for its architecture, food, gardens, and vibrant traditions.

Badshahi Mosque

Built in 1673 by Emperor Aurangzeb, the Badshahi Mosque is one of the largest mosques in the world and a symbol of Mughal architecture.

Lahore Fort

Lahore Fort is a magnificent structure containing beautiful palaces, gardens, and halls built during the Mughal era.

Shalimar Gardens

Shalimar Gardens are famous for their fountains, terraces, and stunning landscape design that attracts visitors from around the world.
